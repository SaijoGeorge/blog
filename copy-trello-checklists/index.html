Copy Trello Checklists

This bookmarklet simplifies the process of copying checklists from one Trello card to another. No need to fumble around with the "Copy items fromâ€¦" dropdown with a gazillion checklists to choose from. The tool is also capable of handling multiple checklists, enabling you to copy all the checklist items to your clipboard and then effortlessly paste them into a different list.


Copy Trello Checklists v1.0

Installation Instructions:

Drag the button above to your bookmarks bar. If you don't see the bookmarks bar, you might need to enable it in your browser settings.

How To Use:

  • When you are on a Trello Card, run the bookmarklet.
  • Checklists will be copied to your clipboard.

Some Limitations:

  • The bookmarklet will only copy checklist items that are visible. Hidden items will not be included in the clipboard.
  • Items that are crossed out will still be copied to the clipboard, but they will not appear as crossed out text when pasted.
