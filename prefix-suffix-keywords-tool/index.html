Bulk Add Prefix or Suffix to multiple lines of text

Bulk add suffix / prefix to keywords is a FREE online tool that will help you add any given prefix or suffix to any batch of keywords that you are working with. Just copy pate the keyword list in the text area , add a suffix or prefix ( or both ) and the tool will generate a new list of keywords with the suffix and/or prefix that you have provided. This will be great tool to have in your arsenal, especially if your work involves adding prefix and/or suffix into each line. If you work with multiple number of keywords at once, this will save you a lot of time.

How do add suffix / prefix to your keywords?

  1. You can click on the ” Clear Prefix Suffix Fields ” button to clear the suffix and prefix fields.
  2. Once you add your suffix or prefix in tot he given area just clcik on ” Add Prefix / Suffix ” and we will do the rest.
  3. Once you generate your result you can click the “Select Text” button and “Ctrl+C” to copy the result to your clipboard.
